eLoc Key

"You Own It – Protect It”

How it works - Plug and Play

  1. Intellectual property owner uses the intuitive CrypKey DLM Utilities to Encrypt and Activate eLocKey prior to distributing it. This makes the files useless to copy as it can’t be opened.
  2. The end user receives the USB Key (flash drive)
  3. The end user plugs in the USBKey and gets a menu of files they can open. Without eLocKey the documents can’t be viewed in any way.
